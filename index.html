The Strategy
CS exams start June 1 — 7 papers in 7 days. College exams run May 13–June 4. Here's how to win both.

Core Rules

01
CS is always priority. Never sacrifice morning CS blocks for college prep.
02
Max 1 hr college prep per night before an exam — old notes + past papers only.
03
June 2 & 4 are double days. CS exam at 9am, college exam at 3pm. Sleep well the night before.
04
Last 30 mins = revision. Every session ends with a review of what was studied.
05
Sundays are half-days. 4 hrs CS max. Rest is not wasted time.
